# Problem Statement Document
## Project Title
### EV Charging Station Finder (Web Application)

## Problem Definition
The significant increase in the electric vehicles (EV) has presented a significant issue due to which the users cannot locate a reliable and convenient charging station. The distribution of the charging stations is not organized, and it lacks real-time opportunities to provide users with an appropriate experience.
Consequently, EV users are subjected to:
•	Problem with locating charging stations in the area.
•	Poor availability information, which is accurate and real-time.
•	Inefficient route planning
•	The development of a higher range anxiety in travel.
## What Problem Are We Solving?
•	The project addresses the issue of weak and untrustworthy access to information on EV charging stations with the help of centralized, real-time, and location-aware platform.
## Who Experiences This Problem? (Target Users)
Its major users are:
•	Electric Vehicles (Daily Commuters, Long-Distance Travelers) owners.
•	Ride Share/Delivery Service Drivers through the use of Electric Vehicles.
•	Fleet Operators intending to embrace Electric Mobility.
•	Tourists/Novice Electric Vehicle Users who are not aware of Charging Infrastructure.
## Why Does This Problem Exist?
This problem exists due to:
•	Classification: There is no central database on EV charging stations.
•	Absence of consistency in the information across various platforms.
•	Failure to fully integrate with updates of availability as well as status. 
•	High adoption of EVs, therefore, there is no awareness of the current infrastructure.
•	Poor interface in the current applications.
## Why Is This Problem Important in Australia?
This issue is particularly important in Australia because:
•	The adoption of EV is growing at an increasing pace due to the sustainability goals.
•	Australia is a country of big distances, thus the range anxiety problem.
•	There is still the development of electric charging infrastructure.
•	Rural and regional locations are problematic in terms of the visibility of the charging infrastructure.
•	Government is encouraging EVs, therefore, support systems should be developed.
## Project Vision
The project vision is to come up with an intuitive, dependable and smart EV Charging Station Finder that: 
•	Offers charging station real-time and precise data.  
•	Navigation It is easy to navigate with geolocation and integration of maps. 
•	Improves the efficiency of travel and decreases the range anxiety. 
•	Helps in changing Australia to sustainable transportation.
