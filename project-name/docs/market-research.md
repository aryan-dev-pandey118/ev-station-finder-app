# Market Analysis Report
## Industry Overview (Australian Context)
### The project operates within the industry of Energy Tech and Smart Mobility that is growing rapidly because of:

• Government policies in favor of electric vehicles.
• Environmental sustainability goals
• Infrastructure EV investment.
### Market Need
The market has a high demand of:

• Centralized EV charging information systems.
• Real-time availability tracking
• Location-based services in optimization of routes.
• Digital platforms that are easy to use and user-friendly to the Australian users.
### Target Market Segmentation
#### The target market includes:

• Primary Market
• Urban EV users in major cities (Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane)
• Daily commuters
• Secondary Market
• Regional and rural EV users
• Long-distance travelers
• Tertiary Market
• Businesses managing EV fleets
• Government and infrastructure planners
### Competitor Overview
#### Existing solutions include:

• Google Maps EV charging integration
• PlugShare and ChargePoint apps
• Limitations of Existing Competitors
• Not fully localized for Australian datasets
• Limited real-time accuracy
• Complex or cluttered user interfaces
• Lack of personalized features
### Market Gaps
#### The gaps that are tackled in the project are as follows:

• Absence of EV charging platform targeted at Australia.
• Poor synchronization of real-time data.
• Poor filtering and customization.
• Poor route planning incorporation.
### Opportunities in Australia
#### Key opportunities include:

• Increased market of EV.
• Supply of EV infrastructure by government.
• The growth of demand of smart mobility solutions.
• The possible collaboration with the energy companies and councils.
###  Challenges and Risks
• External API reliability of data.
• Quick infrastructure transformation in EV.
• Competition with international platforms.
• Technical issues of real-time data integration.
###  Value Proposition
The EV Charging Station Finder will:

• Provide real time, localized and precise data.
• Offer a user-friendly and easy usability.
• Minim range anxiety and travel inefficiency.
• Encourage sustainable transport ecosystem in Australia.
